ABOUT KD TENT HOUSE

Creating Beautiful Weddings & Unforgettable Celebrations Since 2016

Since 2016, KD Tent House has been one of the leading wedding decorators and event planners in Kangra, Himachal Pradesh. We specialize in wedding decoration, luxury tent arrangements, engagement ceremonies, birthday parties, corporate events, stage decoration, floral setups, lighting, catering, and complete event management. Our experienced team focuses on creativity, quality, and attention to detail to transform every celebration into a memorable experience. From intimate gatherings to grand weddings, we deliver elegant décor and seamless event execution tailored to your vision.
